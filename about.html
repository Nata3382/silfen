THE UNIVERSE OF SILFEN

SILFEN is a danish accessory brand enstablished in 2013.
The bramd is characterized by high quality products created in genuine materials. The primary focus is on designing products that reflect the trends of each season, but timeless classics are also a part of the portfolio.
Behind SILFEN stand the siblings Daniel and Celine SIlfen. Both have a deep passion for danish design and growing up their childhood was filled with beautiful bags. They love to build up the brands DNA with a new and playful aesthetic towards the key accessory - the bag.

In the future, SILFEN is working on colorful designs in different kinds of material. They love to play with textures and a wide range of materials in order to challenge their own perceptioon of creating new styles.
Designer Daniel Silfen is never afraid of trying new combinations in order to continuously developing the brand.
The aim with the brand is to offer high qualoity products in a thought through design at reasonable prices.
